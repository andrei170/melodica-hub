Research Snapshot

+

You have the hard part already: ~27 branches across the UAE, KHDA approval, an exam center, and a brand families trust since 2013. Demand is not your problem. Turning local demand into booked trials, on purpose, at every branch, is.

The wedge: acquisition runs on brand, organic, and walk-in. There is no paid engine deliberately filling class capacity, and the one trial path you have is a callback form, not a booking funnel. A parent who searches "piano lessons near me", clicks, and has to wait for a call back is a booked student you are losing.

The play: lead with paid ads to fill the branches, and make them convert. Per-branch Google and Meta ads pointed at a real trial-booking funnel, with per-branch local SEO underneath so each location ranks organically too and every ad lands somewhere relevant.

One honest note: the build keeps your scale claims (student and review counts, "largest in GCC") off the public copy and frames your track record on what is verifiable (since 2013, KHDA-approved, exam center, ~27 branches). No invented testimonials. The trial is never called "free" because that is yours to confirm.
